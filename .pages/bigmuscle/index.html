Having Trouble logging in?
If you log in but when you try to do something on the site it says you are NOT logged in. 

1 - Check that your date and time on your computer is set correct to today's date. If it is not set correctly then the cookies could be getting deleted because your computer thinks they are old or out of date.
 

2 - Try cleaning your browser CACHE. Here are the two most requests browsers that have this problem.

 

Microsoft Internet Explorer

From the Tool Bar click TOOLS -> INTERNET OPTIONS, on the page that shows up about half way down click the DELETE FILES button. This will clean up your browser cache, If you have a large hard drive and have never done this, it can take 5 or more minutes to finish. So may I suggest a glass of water while you are waiting.

AOL 5,6,7 & 8

From the Tool Bar click SETTINGS -> PREFERENCES -> INTERNET PROPERTIES (WWW): on the page that shows up about half way down click the DELETE FILES button. This will clean up your browser cache, If you have a large hard drive and have never done this, it can take 5 or more minutes to finish. So may I suggest a strong drink while you are waiting.
